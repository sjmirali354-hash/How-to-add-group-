How to Add Your Group

Follow these simple steps to submit your group link correctly.

Step 1: Select Platform

Choose the platform where your group is available, such as WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook, Messenger, or any other supported platform.

Step 2: Enter Group Name

Write a short, clear, and easy-to-understand group name.

Example: Jobs Updates, Study Group, Business Community

Step 3: Copy Group Invite Link

Open your selected platform, go to your group, and copy the group invite link.

Step 4: Paste Group Link

Paste the copied invite link in the group link field.

Step 5: Submit Group

Check your details carefully, then tap the Submit button.

Important Notes

Please add only valid and active group links.

Do not submit fake, expired, adult, illegal, or spam links.

Groups that violate our rules may be removed without notice.
